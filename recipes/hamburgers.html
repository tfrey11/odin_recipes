Hamburger Recipe

Description

Delicious juicy hamburgers the whole family will love! Easy and fast to make, perfect for any busy family.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger Buns
  • Pre-made hamburger patties
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayo
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat grill to 400 degrees
  2. Place hamburger patties on grill
  3. Grill hamburger patties for 4 min per side for medium (add or subtract time to cook to preferred tastes)
  4. Place hamburger buns on plates
  5. Place hamburger patties on buns
  6. Add condiments of choice on burger
  7. Enjoy your delicious burger!
